title: Organize Your Project Docs
description: How to create folders for better documentation structure.
---

## Overview

Organizing docs in folders keeps things clear.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a folder">
    Click "New Folder" in your project sidebar.
    Name it (e.g. "Guides" or "API").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move pages into folders">
    Drag and drop pages into your folder, or use the page menu > "Move to folder".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reorder folders">
    Drag folders up or down to arrange sections as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
